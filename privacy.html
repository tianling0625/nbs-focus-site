NBS Focus Privacy Policy

NBS Focus does not collect, store, sell, or share personal data.

Timer settings and completed session records are stored locally on the user's device.

Optional notification permission is used only to send timer alerts when enabled by the user.

Optional Screen Time permission is used only for Focus Lock features enabled by the user.

We do not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking.
